<h3>What is Beefy Slider</h3>

Beefy Slider is a jQuery slider that is great for showing multiple images on your site and is maybe the tastiest slider you have ever tried.

Beefy Slider comes with a number of options including custom links, custom text fields, color control etc.

Beefy Slider is beautiful responsive as well and supports up to 20 images.

Sounds tasty? <strong>Buy Beefy! :)</strong>
